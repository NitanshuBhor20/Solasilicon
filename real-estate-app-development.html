MLS (Multiple Listing Service)

We help enhance your real estate platform by integrating popular Multiple Listing Service (MLS) systems in your region. This includes aligning your data with MLS standards, automating listing updates, and incorporating CRM tools to streamline workflows and improve lead management efficiency.

📡

IDX (Internet Data Exchange)

We help you power your real estate app by integrating with your local MLS through IDX, giving users seamless access to a vast and up-to-date property database. By connecting with trusted IDX providers like IDX Broker or iHomefinder, we ensure smooth integration that complies with MLS guidelines. While attracting agents can expand your reach, we focus on strengthening your platform’s core value proposition for long-term success.

🚚

RETS (Real Estate Transaction Standard)

We bring strong expertise in working with RETS along with multiple IDX providers to ensure smooth and reliable data integration. Our team carefully adheres to industry standards while efficiently transferring MLS data into your platform. We transform complex listing feeds into clean, structured, and easy-to-understand property information, making your data both accurate and user-friendly.
