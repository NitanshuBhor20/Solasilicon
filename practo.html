What technologies are used in MeetDoc?

MeetDoc uses advanced technologies including Flutter, React, Node.js, Laravel, WebRTC, Firebase, MySQL, and secure payment gateway integrations.

Does the platform support video consultation?

Yes. MeetDoc supports secure real-time video consultations, audio calls, and live messaging between doctors and patients.

Can the UI design be customized?

Absolutely. Complete UI/UX customization, branding, layouts, themes, and additional feature integrations are supported.

Does MeetDoc support multiple payment gateways?

Yes. Stripe, Razorpay, PayPal, and many other global payment gateways can be integrated easily.

Is there an admin dashboard included?

Yes. A complete admin management dashboard is included for managing doctors, appointments, analytics, payments, and reports.
