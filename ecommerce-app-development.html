Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)

Offer your customers flexible payment options with Buy Now, Pay Later solutions—one of the most preferred choices in modern eCommerce. This feature allows users to purchase products instantly and pay in installments over time or within a fixed period, improving affordability and boosting conversions. With secure integration of BNPL and digital wallet solutions, you can enhance purchasing flexibility and make shopping more accessible for a wider audience.

📡

Integration of Blockchain in eCommerce

Blockchain technology enhances eCommerce platforms by improving transparency, traceability, and security across the supply chain. It helps verify product authenticity, prevent fraudulent transactions, and track goods in real time from origin to delivery. Additionally, blockchain enables faster, more secure payments with reduced delays and disputes, creating a more reliable and efficient shopping experience for both businesses and customers.

📡

Headless Commerce

Headless commerce is a modern, decoupled architecture that separates the front-end user experience from the back-end system. By connecting both layers through APIs, it enables greater flexibility, faster performance, and seamless multi-platform experiences. In multi-vendor eCommerce apps, it allows sellers to create highly customized storefronts aligned with their brand, improving user experience and unlocking new revenue opportunities.

🚚

Try Before You Buy (AR/VR)

Augmented Reality enables customers to visualize products in real-world settings—like placing furniture in their home or trying on makeup, sunglasses, and clothing—before making a purchase. This increases buying confidence and reduces returns. With Virtual Reality, users can experience immersive product interactions such as exploring cars or vacation homes, creating a more engaging and satisfying shopping journey.

B2B eCommerce

B2B eCommerce platforms are designed to handle large-scale transactions with complex pricing, shipping, and logistics needs. A dedicated web portal and mobile app help streamline operations by enabling features like custom pricing, bulk orders, freight management, net payment terms, purchase orders, and contract handling—all within a seamless digital experience.

🏬

B2C eCommerce

B2C eCommerce platforms focus on delivering a seamless shopping experience directly to end customers. With multiple vendors, it includes features like seller dashboards, independent order fulfillment, third-party shipping and tracking integrations, product management tools, and seller-specific promotions. The entire system is designed to ensure a smooth, customer-centric buying experience from browsing to delivery.

💼

C2C eCommerce

C2C eCommerce platforms enable users to buy and sell products directly with each other, often focusing on resale and second-hand goods. These platforms emphasize quick communication, easy listings, and smooth transactions while ensuring trust and safety. Strong verification systems and fraud prevention measures are essential to maintain authenticity and protect both buyers and sellers from scams.

📰

D2C eCommerce

D2C eCommerce eliminates intermediaries and connects brands directly with customers, requiring strong control over every transaction and interaction. It focuses on building long-term customer relationships through consistent branding, personalized marketing, and customized product offerings. This approach enhances overall user experience while enabling better engagement, trust, and brand loyalty.

🌤️

Subscription eCommerce

Subscription-based eCommerce involves recurring deliveries or services from a single brand, such as monthly product supplies, digital services, or memberships. This model requires efficient management of recurring orders, along with key performance metrics like churn rate, MRR, and ARR, ensuring smooth operations, customer retention, and consistent revenue growth.

❤️

Live eCommerce

Live eCommerce is transforming online shopping by combining real-time video streaming with product selling. Sellers can host live sessions to showcase products, explain features in detail, and interact directly with customers. This real-time engagement builds trust, increases user involvement, and significantly improves conversion rates.

Omnichannel eCommerce

Modern customers shop across multiple touchpoints, including websites, mobile apps, social media, physical stores, and even emerging digital platforms. Omnichannel eCommerce ensures a seamless and consistent experience by synchronizing inventory, pricing, promotions, and branding across all channels. This unified approach enhances customer satisfaction, strengthens brand presence, and unlocks new revenue opportunities across every platform.

Voice Commerce

Voice commerce enables users to search, browse, and purchase products using voice assistants like Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, or mobile voice commands. Although still emerging, it is rapidly growing and expected to become a key shopping channel in the near future. It offers convenience, faster interactions, and enhanced accessibility, making it a valuable addition for modern eCommerce businesses.
