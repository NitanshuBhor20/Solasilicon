Designing Healthcare Apps with Compliance at the Core

We develop healthcare applications that meet global healthcare regulations and interoperability standards, ensuring security, compliance, and seamless healthcare data exchange.

🛡️

HIPAA (USA)

We develop healthcare applications that comply with HIPAA regulations, ensuring the highest standards of patient data privacy and security. Our solutions include robust safeguards to protect sensitive health information, prevent unauthorized access, and support secure data sharing across healthcare systems and networks.

🔄

HL7

We follow HL7 (Health Level Seven International) standards to enable seamless and secure exchange of healthcare information between systems. This ensures better interoperability, accurate data sharing, and improved communication across hospitals, clinics, and healthcare applications.

🔗

FHIR

We develop healthcare applications following FHIR (Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resources) standards to enable secure and seamless exchange of Electronic Health Records (EHRs). Our solutions support interoperability across healthcare systems, mobile applications, and digital health platforms, ensuring efficient access to accurate patient information.

🖼️

DICOM

We develop healthcare solutions that support DICOM (Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine) standards, enabling secure storage, transmission, and access to medical images. This ensures seamless integration of imaging data, improves diagnostic accuracy, and enhances patient care across healthcare systems.

✔️

FDA (USA)

We develop healthcare software with FDA compliance considerations in mind, ensuring solutions are designed to meet safety, quality, and regulatory requirements. Our applications support accurate clinical workflows, informed decision-making, and reliable healthcare outcomes.

⚙️

IEC 62304

We develop healthcare software in alignment with IEC 62304 standards, following structured processes for software development, risk management, testing, and documentation. This ensures high-quality, reliable, and compliant medical device software that meets industry best practices.

📋

FDA Regulations

We develop healthcare software with FDA regulatory requirements in mind, ensuring solutions are designed to meet safety, quality, and compliance standards. Our applications support clinical workflows, healthcare decision-making, and patient care while adhering to industry best practices for secure and reliable performance.

🌍

MDR (EU)

We develop healthcare applications with a strong focus on MDR (Medical Device Regulation) compliance, ensuring high standards of safety, quality, and performance for digital health solutions. Our approach helps healthcare organizations meet European regulatory requirements while delivering secure, reliable, and patient-focused applications.

📄

EHR Certification

We develop healthcare solutions that support EHR certification requirements, ensuring compliance with industry standards for functionality, security, and interoperability. Our applications enable seamless health record management, secure data exchange, and improved connectivity across healthcare systems.
